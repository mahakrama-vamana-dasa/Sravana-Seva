# Text
> etan me janma loke ’smin  
> mumukṣūṇāṁ durāśayāt  
> prasaṅkhyānāya tattvānāṁ  
> sammatāyātma-darśane
# Translation
My appearance in this world is especially to explain the philosophy of Sāṅkhya, which is highly esteemed for self-realization by those desiring freedom from the entanglement of unnecessary material desires.
# Key Points
- #BG2_18: `antavanta ime dehā; nityasyoktāḥ śarīriṇaḥ`
- #Kapila himself is declaring the reason behind his #incarnation. 
- The #purpose of the #material_world is to #rectify the #controlling tendency of #jiva(s) and change to consciousness of #surrender. 
- When the #mind is constantly disturbed then only will we #transcend from material #mental_bliss level to #transcendental_bliss. Only under such condition will one #surrender unto the #lotus_feet. 
- If we have to create hankering for #transcendental_bliss, then we must first realize that this #material_world is #duhkha_alaya. 
- We need to #realize that no #human can give us #unconditional_love. 
- Our #nature is seeking #sac_cid_ananda, whether in #conditioned_state or #liberated_state. 
- One desiring to execute #pure_devotion must be on the #liberated_state. 
- #Hare_Krsna_Mahamantra makes one #detached from the #material_world. One is able to realize the #transient nature of this world. 
- #BG - #tattva_darsi sees the #context, background #purpose, #function of everything. If he see a #mic, he sees that it is in a #lecture_hall, used for #SB lecture.
- #BG2_13: `dehino ’smin yathā dehe`
- If two pieces are stuck together with a bonding or adhesive #chemical, then we need another #chemical or external force to break that bonding. Currently, the #matter has bonded, entangled the #spirit. Just having knowledge of how to separate the bondage is like #Sankhya. But the #force required to actually break the #bondage can't be derived just by #philosophy. One need to do #bhakti to break the #bondage. 
- Because we are #eternal, thus, #sat can never be separated from us.
