# Text
> antaḥ puruṣa-rūpeṇa  
> kāla-rūpeṇa yo bahiḥ  
> samanvety eṣa sattvānāṁ  
> bhagavān ātma-māyayā
# Translation
By exhibiting His potencies, the Supreme Personality of Godhead adjusts all these different elements, keeping Himself within as the #Supersoul and without as time.
# Key Points
- #Paramatma's rupa is that of an #enjoyer or #purusa within everyone. 
	- One who sees as mentioned above sees that each #body is a #temple for there is #Visnu residing in each #body. 
- Lord is present without as #Kala or #time. 
- #Kala is #insurmountable. #Krsna never comes under the control of #time but is the #controller of #time. 
- #jiva(s) have #fruitive_mentality. It want to work for a #fruit and #enjoy it. 
- #Paramatma is #Upadrasta or #witness - #BG13_
	- also, He sanctions our various #desire(s) but at our own #risk. Like if someone #desire(s) to do murder, crime, etc., then sanction is coming from #anumanta or #Paramatma. 
	- initially He tries to give us #intuition to not do #abominable acts but finally when the #jiva doesn't listen to the #conscience, then He sanctions. 
- In a rented house we can't do changes as we like without taking permission from the owner. We can't add staircase, etc. without permission. 
	- Similarly, this world is owned by the Lord. 
	- So, we should utilize the property of Lord in such a way that pleases Him. 
- #BG18_66 - sarva dharman 
- One can see the Supreme Lord by following the #vani of #guru.
