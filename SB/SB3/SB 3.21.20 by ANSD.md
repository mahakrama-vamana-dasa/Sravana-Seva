# Text
> naitad batādhīśa padaṁ tavepsitaṁ  
> yan māyayā nas tanuṣe bhūta-sūkṣmam  
> anugrahāyāstv api yarhi māyayā  
> lasat-tulasyā bhagavān vilakṣitaḥ

# Translation
> My dear Lord, although it is not Your desire, You manifest this creation of gross and subtle elements just for our sensual satisfaction. Let Your causeless mercy be upon us, for You have appeared before us in Your eternal form, adorned with a splendid wreath of tulasi leaves.

# Key Points

- [[Krsna]] is not willing to create the [[Material nature]] but still He creates for [[Jiva]]s. 
- In the [[Spiritual world]], everyone is rendering [[Devotional service]] to [[Krsna]]. 
- When a [[Jiva]] becomes [[envious]] of [[Krsna]], then we come to the [[Material world]]. 
- In the [[Spiritual world]], the [[gopa]]s are always trying to decorate [[Krsna]] and [[Balarama]] with [[flowers]]. 
- In the [[Spiritual world]], there is [[Service attitude]] while in the [[Material world]] there is [[Master attitude]] or [[Lording attitude]]. 
- The [[mood]] in the [[Spiritual world]] is becoming [[servant]] of [[servant]] of… 
- A [[servant leader]] is a person who is not a tinge of expectation of receiving [[service]] from anyone. He wants to serve everyone. 
- [[BG 9.1]] - one should be not [[envious]] of [[Krsna]]. 
- BG avajanati mam mudha. 
- We should give up #fancy #manufactured engagements. 
- Any work we do that doesn't invoke our [[Love of Godhead]], [[rati]] towards [[Krsna]] is but #useless_labor - srama eva hi kevalam - SB
- [[SB 5.5.26]] was quoted - we can offer respect to [[Krsna]] by offering respect to all [[Jiva]]s.
