# Key Points
- Distinction between #deva and #Asura is mentioned. 
	- #Asura(s) want to become one with #God.
- #BG4_8: paritranaya sadhunam
- #vidhi-kara means one who follows the #vidhi. This is the name of #demigod(s). 
- #BG7_7: no #authority is superior to #Krsna. 
- #God is one. There can't be two gods. #asama #anurdhva. 
- #Adi5_142: there is only one #master or #Isvara, rest all are just #servant(s). 
- #BS5_46: potency of all the candles is same but still there is a primary #candle that lit the others. 
- #Expansion(s) of #Krsna are explained
	- #Svamsa: #Visnu-Tattva
	- #Vibhinnamsa: #jiva including #Brahma and #Siva,  #SB11_5_33
- #HKM is #incarnation of #Krsna. #HKM is not different from #Krsna and #Caitanya. 
- #Madhya17_: abhinnatva nama naminah 
- #Antya20_12: `ceto darpan marjanam`
	- Extinguishing the #blazing_fire of the #material_world. 
- Every #incarnation of #Krsna is very pleasing, full of #bliss, i.e., ruci avatara. 
- Old #gold is same as modern #gold. Both have same number of protons and electrons.  Similarly, there is no difference between the opinion of older #devotee(s) and newer #devotee(s). 
- If #Prahlada is such a great #devotee, then why is he addressing him as #Asura, asura-vara, etc.? 
	- #SB5_5_18: Pita na sa syat
	- One reject a #demonic #guru, #pita, #mata, etc. 
	- A person who is not a #devotee can't become a #guru. 
	- pathana pāthana yajana yājana dana pratigraha
	- How to become a #guru? Yara dekho tare kaho krsna upadesa
- #Prabhupada recalls the pastime of #BSST ordering to kill a #snake.
