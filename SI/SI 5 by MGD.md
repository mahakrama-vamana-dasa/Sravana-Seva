> tad ejati tan naijati  
> tad dūre tad v antike  
> tad antar asya sarvasya  
> tad u sarvasyāsya bāhyataḥ

**Translation (by SP):** The Supreme Lord walks and does not walk. He is far away, but He is very near as well. He is within everything, and again He is outside of everything.
# Vedārka Dīdhiti Commentary

> tad ejati tat ātma-tattvaṁ ejati chalati. tan-naijati ejati tad-dūre varttate. tad vantike varttate. tat antarasya sarvasya. tadu tat asya viśvasya sarvasya bāhyataḥ tiṣṭhati.

(“The _ātmā-tattva_ (Paramātmā)  is both moving and non-moving. He is both far and near, present within the universe and outside of it.”)
