# Vedārka Dīdhiti Commentary by BVT

> anejat na ejat ejṛkaspane niścalaṁ iti arthaḥ. tat ātma-tattvaṁ niścalaṁ-ekaṁ. Manasaḥ javīyaḥ. devā indriyāṇi tat na āpanūvan prāpta-vastuḥ. yataḥ pūrvamarṣata pūrvaṁ eva gataṁ tat dhāvataḥ drataṁ gacchataḥ anyān manaḥ prabhṛtīn. atyeti atikrāmati. tat tiṣṭhat. tasmin ātmani mātariśvā vāyuḥ. apaḥ karmāṇi dadhāti dhāvayati.

**(“The** **Paramātmā and the _ātmā_ are stationary, they are individual, and they are faster than the mind.** **All the senses cannot grasp them since the _ātmā_ precedes the senses.** **While the mind, etc., may run, the _ātmā_ always excels them.** **When the _ātmā_ remains inactive, Vāyu performs activities on his behalf.”)**
