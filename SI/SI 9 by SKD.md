# Text and Translation
> andhaṁ tamaḥ praviśanti ye ‘vidyām upāsate  
> tato bhūya iva te tamo ya u vidyāyām ratāḥ


# Vedārka Dīdhiti Commentary
> ye avidyāyāṁ upāsate te andhaṁ tamaḥ praviśanti. 
> ye u tu vidyāyāṁ ratāh te tataḥ tasmāt adhikataraṁ tamaḥ praviśanti.

**One situated in ignorance enters into a realm of complete darkness. And one engaged in knowledge enters a darker place than that.**

# Key Points
- vidya rata of this sloka = veda vada rata of #BG2_42.
	- this is same as #mudha of #BG7_15.
	- #Vedic_Literature can be understood only through #parampara.
	- `tad vijnanartham gurum evabhigacchet`
- mayaya apahrta jnana 
	- `aham graha upasana`: a nonsense method in which one worships himself by identifying himself with the #Absolute_Truth
	- `mayavada bhasya sunile haya sarvanasa`
