# Text
> hiraṇmayena pātreṇa 
> satyasyāpihitaṁ mukham  
> tat tvaṁ pūṣann apāvṛṇu 
> satya-dharmāya dṛṣṭaye

# Translation
O my Lord, sustainer of all that lives, Your real face is covered by Your dazzling effulgence. Kindly remove that covering and exhibit Yourself to Your pure devotee.
# Vedārka Dīdhiti Commentary
> hiraṇmayena jyotir-mayena pātrena satyasya parama-tattvasya mukhaṁ apahitaṁ ācchāditaṁ. 
> satya-dharmāya dṛṣṭaye upalabdhaye he pūṣan tat pidhānaṁ tvaṁ apāvṛṇu.

**“Paramātmā’s form is concealed by a covering of light. O Sūrya! Remove that covering so Your true nature and the principle of the _ātmā_ can manifest.”**
